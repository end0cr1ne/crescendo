Fr. Conceicao Rodrigues College of Engineering

Fr. Conceicao Rodrigues College of Engineering (CRCE), popularly known as Fr. Agnel Bandra, is an un-aided, minority, non-autonomous institute affiliated to the University of Mumbai offering B.E. degrees in Computers, Electronics, Production and Information Technology branches.
